About Me

I fell in love with technology back in high school. The fact that technology runs the world was enough to convince me to join the tech world. I decided to take a Bachelor of Business Information Technology at the university. I love programming since it is the main way that one can make huge contributions in tech world. At the university, I was introduced to different programming languages like C, C++ and Java. After completing campus I felt that I needed more skills in programming and I therefore joined Moringa School. At Moringa, I learnt HTML, CSS, JavaScript and specialized in full stack development. For the five months I was at Moringa, I worked on a lot of applications, both applications we developed as a team of four and applications that I developed individually, that helped me grasp whatever I was learning. I worked in a team of five to develop an application to facilitate equities trading for Sanlam Investments Limited.
